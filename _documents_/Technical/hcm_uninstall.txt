To remove the HaZardModding Co-Op Mod for Star Trek: Elite Force II
from your 'EF2\base' folder, follow these instructions.

1.	Go to the 'EF2\base' folder of your Game.

2.	Open the file zzzzzz_co-op_hzm_script_mod.pk3 with a Archiver
	then go into the folder _tools_ and open the file gamex86.zip
	extzract the file gamex86.dll and copy it into your games base
	directory, overwritring the existing newer file.

3.	Delete these files from your base folder to remove the other
	HaZardModding Coop Mod components:
zzzzzz_co-op_hzm_script_mod.pk3
zzzzzz_co-op_hzm_script_mod_vlp.pk3
zzzzzz_co-op_hzm_script_mod_dialogENG.pk3
zzzzzz_co-op_hzm_script_mod_dialogDEU.pk3
